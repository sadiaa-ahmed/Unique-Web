Quran Reading

Our Quran Reading class is dedicated to teaching children how to recite the Quran. The aim is to develop their ability to accurately identify the words of the Quran and enhance their recitation skills. Children will be taught to read the words through easy and engaging methods, allowing them to better understand the verses of the Quran. This experience will not only strengthen their connection with Allah but also play a significant role in their spiritual development. Regular recitation will increase their ability to comprehend the message of the Quran and foster a deep love for it in their hearts.
