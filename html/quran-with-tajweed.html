Course Outline

Quran With Tajweed

Our Quran with Tajweed course is tailored for adults and teenagers looking to improve their Quranic recitation. This course emphasizes the correct pronunciation and articulation of Arabic letters while teaching the essential rules of Tajweed. Through structured lessons, students will learn to recite the Quran beautifully and confidently, deepening their connection with the text.
