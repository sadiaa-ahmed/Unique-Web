Course Outline

Recitation of the Quran

Our Recitation of the Quran course is tailored for adults and teenagers, focusing on improving their skills in reading and reciting the Quran accurately. Through structured lessons and personalized feedback, participants will learn proper pronunciation, intonation, and rhythm. This course enhances not only technical skills but also fosters a deeper connection to the meanings of the verses, enriching their spiritual journey and appreciation for the Quran in daily life.
