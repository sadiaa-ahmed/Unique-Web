Course Outline

Norani Qaida

This course is designed for beginners who wish to learn how to read the Quran with proper Tajweed. It provides a foundational understanding of the Arabic alphabet and introduces students to the basic rules of Quranic recitation. Starting with the Noorani Qaida, students will gradually learn to read and pronounce Arabic letters, words, and phrases correctly. By the end of the course, learners will be able to read the Quran fluently with proper pronunciation. Alongside Quranic reading, students will also be introduced to important Islamic practices such as:

  • 1.Masnoon Dua
  • 2.Six Kalimas
