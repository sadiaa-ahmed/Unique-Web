Course Outline

Islamic Manners

Our Islamic Manners course teaches children the importance of good behavior and etiquette in everyday life. Through engaging lessons and interactive activities, we help them understand respect, kindness, and empathy towards others. They will learn essential principles like honesty, patience, and gratitude, which build strong character. By instilling these manners, we foster positive relationships with peers and elders, nurturing a sense of community and compassion. This course aims to enhance both their social skills and spiritual growth, shaping them into responsible and caring individuals.
